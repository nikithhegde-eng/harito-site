## Homepage Intro

Harito helps shoppers discover curated fashion, compare discounts, and find outfit ideas from trusted fashion sources. Our goal is to make style discovery feel cleaner, faster, and more useful.

## About Us

Harito is a curated fashion discovery platform built for shoppers who want a simpler way to explore clothing, footwear, and accessories across different fashion sources.

We focus on clean product discovery, discount-first browsing, and outfit guidance so buyers can make better style decisions. Harito is currently in preview while we build vendor partnerships, improve inventory access, and prepare approved checkout, delivery, and return workflows.

For partnerships or business inquiries, contact contact@harito.in.

## Return And Refund Policy Draft

Harito is currently building its vendor and fulfillment process.

If your order is fulfilled directly by Harito, return eligibility will depend on the product condition, return window, and reason for return.

If your purchase is completed through an external vendor or brand website, that vendor's return and refund policy will apply. Customers should follow the return instructions provided by the original seller.

To request support, email contact@harito.in with:

- Order ID
- Buyer email
- Product name
- Purchase date
- Reason for return
- Photos, if the product is damaged or incorrect
