Affiliate roadmap

How Harito can earn through links

Harito should use affiliate links first. This avoids handling payments, stock, shipping, or returns while still allowing the site to earn commission from approved partner programs.

01

Affiliate approval

Join approved brand, marketplace, or affiliate networks and store the allowed link rules.

02

Catalog source

Use partner feeds when available, or curated links with accurate price and discount checks.

03

Partner checkout

Send shoppers to the partner site through tracked links so the seller handles payment and fulfillment.

04

Commission report

Track clicks, conversions, payout status, and top-performing products from each affiliate network.

05

Support handoff

Make it clear that order tracking, delivery, returns, and refunds are handled by the final seller.

06

Direct commerce later

Revisit Harito checkout only after supply, legal, payment, delivery, and return responsibilities are clear.

Revenue routes

Affiliate terms to confirm

To use affiliate product links responsibly, Harito should confirm link usage, catalog rules, image rights, commission percentage, payout timing, and buyer support ownership.

Tracking links

Use approved affiliate URLs, campaign IDs, disclosure text, and network reporting rules.

Catalog feed

Request product title, price, stock status, category, destination URL, and update frequency.

Commission payout

Confirm commission rate, cookie window, payout threshold, payout timing, and reversal rules.

Seller fulfillment

Confirm that checkout, delivery tracking, cancellations, returns, and refunds stay with the seller.

Returns and support

Document where buyers should go for order help after leaving Harito through a partner link.

Brand usage rules

Confirm logo, product image, discount display, AI recommendation, and ad-copy permissions before publishing.
